# Coffee-Shop-Sales

📌 Project Overview
This project presents an interactive Coffee Shop Sales Dashboard built in Microsoft Excel to analyze retail sales data and provide business insights. The dashboard answers the business questions defined in the project requirements and helps stakeholders monitor sales performance using interactive visualizations. The project objective is to analyze retail sales data to generate actionable insights for improving coffee shop performance.

📈 Business Questions Solved<br>
How do sales vary by day of the week and hour of the day?<br>
What are the peak sales hours?<br>
How do sales vary across store locations?<br>
Which products generate the highest revenue?<br>
What is the average bill per customer?<br>
Which product categories contribute the most to sales?<br>
How are orders distributed by product size?<br>


📌 Key Insights<br>
Morning hours (around 8 AM–10 AM) have the highest order quantity.<br>
Coffee products contribute the largest share of sales.<br>
Store performance is relatively balanced across locations.<br>
Regular and Large sizes account for the majority of orders.<br>
Barista Espresso is the top-selling product by revenue.<br>
